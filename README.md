# 🌉 VendorBridge

**Simplifying Procurement & Vendor Management**

> Built as a solution to the assigned problem statement for the **odoo x KSV Hackathon 2026** (June 6th, 2026).

---

## 📖 About the Project
Managing vendors, requesting quotes, and handling approvals can be a huge headache for any organization. **VendorBridge** is here to fix that. 

Our vision is to bring all your procurement operations into one easy-to-use digital platform. Instead of getting lost in endless emails and spreadsheets, VendorBridge helps you manage everything from finding vendors to generating the final invoice—all in one place.

## 🚀 The Problem We Solve
Traditional procurement is full of manual work, slow approvals, and scattered communication. VendorBridge removes these inefficiencies by providing:
- Structured, easy-to-follow workflows.
- A central hub for talking to vendors and comparing their quotes.
- Real-time tracking so you always know exactly where an order stands.

## ✨ Key Features
Here's what you can do with VendorBridge:
- **Vendor Management:** Easily register vendors, track their status, and keep their contact and tax details organized.
- **Request for Quotation (RFQ):** Create detailed requests for products or services and assign them to vendors with clear deadlines.
- **Quotation Submission & Comparison:** Vendors can submit their prices directly. You can then view them side-by-side to find the best deal based on price, delivery time, and ratings.
- **Approval Workflow:** No more chasing managers for signatures. Send requests for approval, track the status, and get notifications instantly.
- **Purchase Orders & Invoices:** Automatically turn approved quotes into official Purchase Orders. Generate, print, or email invoices with calculated taxes and totals.
- **Activity Tracking & Analytics:** Keep an eye on your spending, monitor vendor performance, and view a complete timeline of all procurement activities.

## 👥 Who Is This For? (User Roles)
VendorBridge is designed for everyone involved in the procurement process:
- **Procurement Officer:** Creates RFQs, compares quotes, and generates orders/invoices.
- **Manager / Approver:** Reviews, approves, or rejects requests and monitors the workflow.
- **Vendor:** Receives invitations, submits quotations, and tracks the status of their requests.
- **Admin:** Manages all users, vendors, and has access to high-level analytics.

## 🔄 How It Works (The Basic Workflow)
1. 📝 **Create RFQ:** The Procurement Officer creates a Request for Quotation.
2. 📬 **Vendors Respond:** Invited vendors receive the request and submit their best quotes.
3. ⚖️ **Compare & Choose:** The procurement team compares all quotes side-by-side to pick the winner.
4. ✅ **Get Approval:** The selected quote goes through the approval workflow.
5. 📄 **Generate PO:** Once approved, an official Purchase Order is automatically created.
6. 🧾 **Invoice & Send:** Generate an invoice from the PO, which can be printed or emailed directly to the vendor.
7. 📊 **Track Everything:** All activities are logged, and performance is tracked in the analytics dashboard.
